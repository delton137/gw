Important Disclaimers

This report is for informational and educational purposes only. It is not a medical diagnosis, clinical test, or substitute for professional medical advice.

The genetic associations shown are based on published research and curated databases. Many associations have small effect sizes and limited predictive value for any individual.

Polygenic risk scores reflect statistical risk relative to a reference population. They do not account for lifestyle, environmental, or non-genetic factors.

Pharmacogenomic results are derived from SNP-based genotyping, which may not detect all functional variants (e.g., copy number variations, structural variants). Star allele calls should be confirmed by clinical-grade testing before making prescribing decisions.

Carrier screening results test only a subset of known pathogenic variants. A "not detected" result does not eliminate carrier risk. Genetic counseling is recommended for family planning decisions.

ClinVar annotations reflect the current state of submitted variant classifications, which may change over time. A "pathogenic" classification does not mean you will develop the associated condition.

Raw genetic data was never stored. This report was generated from pre-computed analysis results.

Data sources include ClinVar (NCBI), PGS Catalog, CPIC, DPWG, gnomAD, SNPedia, and published GWAS studies.
